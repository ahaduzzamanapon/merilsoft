Diversity Statement

Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI)

We are committed to creating a work environment that is inclusive for all employees, regardless of race, ethnicity, gender, age, religion, sexual orientation, ability status, or any other aspect of identity. We strive to provide equal opportunities for all employees and eliminate discrimination in the workplace.

Our commitment to diversity extends beyond the walls of our organization and into our communities, where we partner with organizations and initiatives that support diversity and inclusion.

We hold ourselves accountable for creating a welcoming and inclusive workplace, and we welcome all employees to bring their whole selves to work. We celebrate the differences that make us unique and believe that these differences are what make our company stronger.

Merilsoft Technology is dedicated to creating a culture of diversity, equity, and inclusion, and we are committed to making it a priority in all of our practices and policies.
